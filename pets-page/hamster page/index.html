Hamster

A hamster is a small, burrowing rodent belonging to the Cricetidae family, which includes various species such as the golden hamster (Mesocricetus auratus) commonly kept as a pet. Hamsters are characterized by their internal cheek pouches and are popular pets due to their small size, relatively clean nature, and ease of care.
