About Aurora

We’re a neighborhood café focused on ethically sourced beans and slow-crafted pastries. Our roasters partner directly with small farms to ensure quality and sustainability. From sunrise espresso to late-afternoon cappuccino, we brew with care.

Sit by the window, browse our seasonal menu, or bring a friend—there’s always a warm seat for good conversation and great coffee.
